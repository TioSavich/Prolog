# Audit log: strategies

Written after [docs/phase5/fact-sheet-strategies.md](fact-sheet-strategies.md). Research goal from the fact-sheet is the cut criterion.

## Cut criterion

> 27 FSM automata model children's arithmetic strategies (Carpenter/Fennema CGI taxonomy via Hackenberg, per attribution in `math/README.md` — not carried in the Prolog sources themselves); 20 Indiana K-3 standards modules cover Kindergarten through Grade 3 and dispatch to those automata. No elaboration graph connects the automata to one another. Fractions remain open: `jason_fsm.pl` and `standard_3_ns_2.pl` exist but are not integrated with the ORR cycle (proposed, not empirically coded).
